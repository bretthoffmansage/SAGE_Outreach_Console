# DESIGN_DIRECTION_BRIEF — AI Campaign Desk

Date: 2026-04-30

## Direction
AI Campaign Desk should feel like a calm, premium internal marketing operating system: warm neutral surfaces, deep navy text/nav, muted indigo accents, rounded cards, subtle shadows, generous spacing, and clear workflow hierarchy.

## Audience-specific design principles
- Bari: simple copy review, no technical clutter, clear approve/edit/rewrite choices.
- Blue: decision cards with why he is needed and what happens next.
- Operators/admins: more operational detail, agent outputs, integration status, and audit trails.

## Current implementation posture
The scaffold applies warm gradients, glass panels, rounded cards, status pills, route-specific dashboards, and non-technical summaries. It is sufficient for MVP scaffold review.
